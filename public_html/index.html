About Me:

I grew up in Albuquerque New Mexico and graduated from Amy Biehl high school in 2019. There I took dual credit classes at CNM, I took business professionalism and Intro to Art. As a graduation requirement I also volunteered for over 100 hours at ECHO, a local food bank for senior citizens. After High school I took some more business and art classes at CNM but soon found out that was not what I wanted to do. I then found Deep Dive Coding through Tech Hire New Mexico and was interested in their Full Stack Web Development boot camp. With the help of Tech Hire I was able to take the 10 week Boot camp in April of 2020 and am graduating in June of 2020. After the boot camp I hope to find a job in front end web development.

Portfolio:

These are some examples of my work I did through out the boot camp. I picked these three specifically, because I feel that they show my progression throughout the camp. Starting with the CSS challenge then assessment 1 and ending with the capstone project.

CSS challenge

The CSS challenge was one of the earlier projects/ challenges we were assigned in the boot camp. We were given a screen shot of a website and the HTML of the site. But the site had no CSS styling. We had to duplicate what the screen shot looked like, using CSS. I think this shows how my CSS has improved throughout the boot camp.

Assessment 1

This was our first assessment in the boot camp. We had to make an HTTP request to an api, and use fetch to display the results in a bootstrap card column. We also had to add some style to the cards using CSS. I was really happy with how this turned out.

Capstone project

The capstone project, is the main project is the boot camp. You are placed in a group and we vote on the projects/ sites we would like to build. My group was 3 people (Kevin Adams, Carl Hernandez and myself). Our project was a mac and cheese ranking website. It ranks all mac and cheese in Albuquerque, which users can vote on. You can also upload your own picture of mac and cheese, which users can also vote on one against another. I'm really happy with the progress my team and I are making on this project.
